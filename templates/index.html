Our Management Team

Dr. Hafeez Rahman Padiyath //Founder Chairman, Emergency Group of Hospitals//

Dr Hafeez Rahman is world-renowned as one of the pioneers in Minimally Invasive Surgery or Laparoscopic Surgical Procedures. After completing MBBS from Calicut Medical College, Kerala in India and joined Govt Medical College at Ahmedabad for his post-graduation. He is a Gold Medalist from Gujarat University. He has worked with renowned Onco surgeons from UK and Australia at Regional Cancer Centre, Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India. He did the first Laparoscopic Hysterectomy in 1992, only 3 years after the first Laparoscopic Hysterestomy in the world was performed in 1989. Over the past 30 years, he has done more than 1,00,000 Laparoscopic Surgeries including 25,000+ Hysterectomies. He is a visiting Laparoscopic Surgeon at various institutes in India and Abroad (Malaysia, Srilanka, UAE and Bangladesh). He introduced Laparoscopy in many states India and neighboring like Sri Lanka and Bangladesh. Being innovative and blessed with special surgical skills including the rare gift of ambidextrous, he has modified several existing surgical techniques, making them cost effective, easier to perform, safer and of greater efficacy. Over the years, he has developed and standardized many unique techniques of his own, which are now diligently followed worldwide. Teaching is his passion and he has trained more than 2000 doctors from different part of the world.
